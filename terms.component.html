DRESS CODE :

  1. Only Non-marketing badminton shoes are permitted inside the court. No barefoot play is permitted.

  2. All players must wear a T-shirt at all times while on the courts. Shorts of jean material are not permitted.

  3. Black soled shoes of any kind are not allowed.

  4. Shoes to be worn on the courts are to be brought into the club and players will change into those shoes prior to entering the court area. Regardless of the footwear type, wet, dirty or shoes that have been worn outside will not be permitted on the courts. Likewise a player who leaves the court to go to the parking lot will not be allowed to wear those shoes back on to the courts.

  5. Street shoes, slippers, flip flops or any kind of footwear not specifically designed for badminton is not permitted.

  6. No Street clothing, Sleeveless T- Shirts, tops with vulgar language or defamatory logos or designs.

  7. Everyone needs to bring a towel and full water bottle.
